## Part 2 — The fast special-card meta (GUIDANCE — not a live crawl)

> Everything in Part 2 is knowledge-based meta guidance, **not** verified live card
> data. Confirm exact ratings, pace, and PlayStyles in-game before you buy.

### Pace isn't one number — it's AcceleRATE
Two players with the same PAC accelerate completely differently. The hidden
**AcceleRATE** archetype (driven by height/agility/strength/acceleration) matters
more than the PAC value:

- **Explosive** — short, agile, bursts away instantly over 2–4 yards. Best for
  **wingers / Inside Forwards** in tight space (think Doku, Barcola, Vini archetype).
- **Lengthy** — tall + fast; slow to start but unstoppable over distance. Best for
  **channel-running strikers, wingbacks, and CBs** (Mbappé, Haaland-lite, Frimpong
  archetype). **Lengthy is the strongest meta archetype** because it wins footraces
  and defends through balls.
- **Controlled** — balanced, no extreme. Fine, never elite for pure speed.

**Rule:** on a special card, a small PAC bump that flips a player to **Explosive**
or **Lengthy** is worth more than +5 raw PAC that leaves them Controlled.

### What makes a special card a *fast* meta card
1. **PAC in the 90s _and_ Explosive/Lengthy** AcceleRATE.
2. A pace-relevant **PlayStyle+**: **Rapid+** (sprint with the ball), **Quick Step+**
   (explosive first step), **Trickster+** / **Technical+** (control at speed).
3. Plays a **pace-leveraging role** (see `04`): Inside Forward, Advanced Forward,
   Attacking Wingback, or a Lengthy CB for a high line.

### Where the fast special cards come from
- **Evolutions** — the most reliable way to *build* a fast meta card from a cheap
  base. Pace/AcceleRATE Evos (e.g. the "Momentum Shift"-type +PAC + **Quick Step+**
  upgrades) turn an 82-rated speedster into a meta starter for free. **Pick a base
  that's already Explosive/Lengthy** so the boost compounds.
- **Promo cards** (Team of the Season, RTTK/road-to cards, etc.) — these stack big
  PAC + PlayStyle+ on already-fast players. The fastest base cards in Part 1 are the
  prime candidates for these.
- **Hero/Icon** cards — many older speedsters get Lengthy/Explosive treatment.

### Practical fast-meta shopping rules
- Start from the **Part 1 list** — buy the special version of a player who's already
  90+ PAC at a pace-relevant position.
- For **wide attack**, prioritise Explosive + Rapid+/Trickster+ (Doku/Barcola/Leão
  archetypes).
- For **CB in a high line** (`02` Gegenpress), get a **Lengthy** defender with 85+
  PAC — it's the single biggest defensive upgrade for a pressing tactic.
- For **wingbacks** (`03` 4-2-2-2 / 3-5-2), Frimpong/Rațiu-type 90+ PAC + stamina
  are ideal special-card targets.
- Don't pay for PAC the role won't use (a Target Forward doesn't need 95 pace).
