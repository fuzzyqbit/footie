# 05 — Player Acquisitions (FUT & Career Mode)

Buying well is a skill. The headline rule for FC 26: **chase PlayStyles+, role
familiarity, and the right body type for the position — not just the overall.**

---

## What actually makes a player good in FC 26

In rough order of in-game impact:

1. **Pace** (Acceleration type especially — see below) — still king in 1v1s both
   ways.
2. **PlayStyles+** (the gold/plus versions) — these are *deterministic* edges:
   Finesse Shot+, Power Shot+, Incisive Pass+, Whipped Pass+, Rapid+, Trickster+,
   Press Proven+, Anticipate+, Jockey+, Aerial+. One relevant **+** often beats 3–4
   overall points.
3. **Role familiarity (+ / ++)** — a natural-fit player moves and defends smarter.
4. **The right "AcceleRATE" archetype** for the role:
   - **Explosive** (short, agile, high acc.) → wingers, dribblers, Inside Forwards.
   - **Lengthy** (tall, fast, strong) → CBs, channel-running strikers, Wingbacks.
   - **Controlled** → balanced all-rounders.
5. **Position-specific cores:** Strikers → Finishing/Composure + Finesse Shot+;
   Wingers → Agility/Dribbling + Acceleration; CMs → Passing + Stamina + Composure;
   CBs → Defending + Pace + Aggression + Height; GKs → Reflexes/Positioning + a
   consistent body type.
6. **Weak Foot / Skill Moves** — 4★/4★ is the comfort baseline for attackers; 5★
   skills only matters if you actually use the moves.

> **Don't** pay a premium for stats you won't use (e.g. a target man's dribbling).
> **Do** pay up for the one or two attributes the role lives on.

## FUT: building a squad that wins

### Chemistry (build it cheaply)
- Chemistry comes from **club + league + nation** links via players being **in
  position**. Plan around **2–3 league/nation cores** so everyone hits 3-chem.
- **Managers** add a league/nation link — use one to glue an otherwise-split squad.
- **Position matters:** a player out of position gives **0 chemistry** and can't
  receive boosts. Only play players where they belong (or apply a Position
  Modifier).

### A sane progression (don't blow your coins on day one)
1. **Early:** cheap meta-league cores (a popular league lets you upgrade piecemeal
   while keeping chem). Spend on **gameplay**, not names.
2. **Mid:** target **affordable PlayStyles+ cards** at the positions that matter
   (a Finesse Shot+ winger, an Incisive Pass+ CM). These carry games.
3. **Late/endgame:** chase **meta SBC/promo cards** with the right AcceleRATE +
   role fit. Buy the *role*, not the badge.

### Market & economy tips
- **Buy low on red-flag days** (right after a big promo drops, or Sunday night when
  Champions ends — fodder/players dip).
- **Sell into hype**, buy into lulls. Player prices breathe with promo cycles.
- Use the transfer **filters** (position, PlayStyle, price ceiling) and **bid**
  during off-peak hours for under-priced BINs.
- **SBCs:** check whether buying the players for an SBC is cheaper than packing
  them — often it is. Do **untradeable fodder** into SBCs first.
- **Don't gamble packs** expecting value; packs are entertainment, the market is
  where you build.

## Career Mode: transfers & squad building

### Scouting & value
- **Buy young (≤21) with high potential** and sell-on value. Use scouting reports;
  prioritise high **potential range** and **wonderkid** flags over current overall.
- **Wage bill discipline:** youth contracts are cheap; don't cripple your budget
  on declining 30+ stars unless you're win-now.
- **Position + role fit:** sign players whose attributes suit the **Role** you'll
  play them in (don't buy a Poacher to play False 9).

### Negotiation tactics
- Trigger interest with **release-clause activations** and **end-of-window
  desperation** (clubs sell cheaper late).
- Use **player+cash** and **loan-to-buy** to land talent you can't afford outright.
- **Loan out** young players for **game time → faster growth**, then reintegrate.

### Development
- Set the right **player role + training focus** so growth funnels into the
  attributes the role needs.
- **Game time is growth** — rotate youngsters into cup games and weaker fixtures.
- Use **dynamic potential**: consistent high performances can push a wonderkid's
  ceiling up.

### A starter buy-list philosophy
1. One **anchor** at CDM/CB (experienced, reliable) to stabilize the spine.
2. Two–three **wonderkids** (W, CM, ST) to grow into stars and bankroll the club.
3. A **backup GK** and squad depth so you can rotate without dropping quality.
4. Sell **fringe/declining** players each window to fund the next wonderkid.

---

## Universal rule
**Identify the role → list the 2–3 attributes/PlayStyles+ that role lives on →
buy the cheapest player that maxes those.** That discipline beats chasing overalls
every single time.
