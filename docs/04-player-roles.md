# 04 — Player Roles & Focuses (FC IQ)

In FC 26 every position is assigned a **Role**, and most roles have a **Focus**
(e.g. Defend / Balanced / Attack) that fine-tunes movement. Roles decide off-ball
runs, defensive positioning, and where a player drifts. A player has a
**familiarity** with each role (basic ↔ "Role+" / "Role++"); using a player in a
role they're **+ / ++** rated for boosts their in-game movement and IQ.

> **Golden rule:** build the lineup around role *chemistry*, not just overalls. A
> 99 striker stuck in a role he can't run is worse than an 84 Poacher who's a
> natural fit.

---

## Goalkeeper
- **Goalkeeper** — stays home. Safe default.
- **Sweeper Keeper** — steps off the line to mop up through balls. Pair with a
  **high defensive line** so the channel behind is covered. Needs a keeper with
  pace/positioning; risky vs lobs if you over-commit.

## Fullbacks / Wingbacks
- **Fullback (Defend/Balanced)** — disciplined, holds the back four. Use one on each
  side when you want defensive security.
- **Wingback (Balanced/Attack)** — overlaps to provide width, recovers reasonably.
  The standard attacking fullback.
- **Attacking Wingback** — lives in the final third; supplies crosses and 2v1s.
  **Pairs with Inside Forwards** (winger comes in, wingback goes outside). Leaves
  space behind — only run on one side, or with cover.
- **Falseback** — tucks inside into midfield in possession to form a back-three /
  extra pivot. Elite for **possession** builds (extra passing angle, protects vs
  counters). Use on the side of your playmaker.

> **Balance tip:** don't run **both** fullbacks as Attacking Wingbacks. Pair one
> attacking side with one defensive/Falseback side, or you'll get countered into
> the open flank every time.

## Central / Defensive Midfield
- **Holding** — sits in front of the back four, screens, rarely leaves. The anchor
  that lets everyone else push. **Almost always run at least one.**
- **Deep-Lying Playmaker (DLP)** — drops to collect and dictate from deep. Your
  metronome in possession builds.
- **Centre Half (in a double pivot)** — even more defensive than Holding; for
  lockdown/closing setups.

## Box-to-Box / Attacking Midfield
- **Box-to-Box (B2B)** — covers ground both ways, arrives late in the box. Engine of
  4-3-3. Needs **stamina + Relentless** to be worth it.
- **Playmaker / Half-Winger** — orchestrates, drifts to find space between lines.
- **Attacking Mid / Classic 10** — sits behind the striker, the focal creative point
  in 4-2-3-1.
- **Shadow Striker** — a 10 that makes aggressive runs **beyond** the striker;
  deadly late-arriving threat in 4-2-2-2 / behind-two setups.

## Wide forwards
- **Winger** — hugs the touchline, beats the fullback, whips crosses. Pairs with an
  **overlapping/attacking** fullback only if you want chaos; usually with a more
  reserved fullback behind.
- **Inside Forward** — cuts inside onto the stronger foot to shoot/combine. **Pairs
  with Attacking Wingbacks** who take the outside lane. The go-to for finesse-shot
  goals.

## Strikers
- **Advanced Forward** — leads the line, runs the channels, presses. The all-round
  striker; good default.
- **Poacher** — lives on the shoulder of the last defender, gambles for tap-ins.
  Lethal with service; invisible without it. Great as the *second* striker.
- **Target Forward** — holds the ball up, wins headers, brings others in. The
  pivot for **Long Ball / Wing Overload** crosses.
- **False 9** — drops between the lines, vacates the CB, creates central space for
  midfield runners. Cornerstone of possession 4-3-3.

## Putting roles together — worked examples

**4-2-3-1 "Lethal Counter":**
GK (Goalkeeper) · LB **Fullback** / RB **Wingback** · CB ×2 **Defender** ·
CDM **Holding** + CDM **Deep-Lying Playmaker** · CAM **Shadow Striker** ·
LW/RW **Inside Forward** · ST **Advanced Forward**.

**4-2-2-2 "Wing Overload":**
GK · LB/RB **Attacking Wingback** · CB ×2 **Defender** · CDM **Holding** +
CDM **DLP** · CAM ×2 (**Inside Forward** + **Shadow Striker**) ·
ST **Target Forward** + ST **Poacher**.

**4-3-3 "Patient Possession":**
GK (**Sweeper Keeper**) · LB **Falseback** / RB **Wingback** · CB ×2 **Defender** ·
CM **DLP** (anchor) + CM ×2 **Box-to-Box / Playmaker** · LW/RW **Inside Forward** ·
ST **False 9**.

## Checklist for a coherent lineup
- [ ] At least **one Holding/anchor** in midfield at all times.
- [ ] **Not both** fullbacks fully attacking.
- [ ] Wide forward role **complements** the fullback behind (Inside Fwd ↔ Attacking
      WB; Winger ↔ reserved FB).
- [ ] Players are at least **Role+** familiar where possible.
- [ ] One **ball-holder up top** (Advanced/Target/False 9) so you can relieve
      pressure — don't field two pure Poachers with no link play.
