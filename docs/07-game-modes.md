# 07 — Strategy by Game Mode

The same tactics apply everywhere, but each mode rewards different priorities.

---

## Ultimate Team (FUT)

### Rivals & Champions (the competitive core)
- **Pick ONE tactic and master it.** Consistency beats variety. Run a forgiving
  shape (4-2-3-1 / 4-3-3 Holding) until your defending is solid.
- **Defend first, attack on the break** — most ranked goals come from counters and
  set pieces, not 30-pass moves.
- **Manage the scoreline with mentality + subs** (see `02`): protect leads with a
  Deep block, chase with attacking mentality + fresh legs.
- **Champions/Weekend League prep:** warm up a few Rivals games first; play **when
  fresh** — fatigue (yours, not the players') loses more games than anything.

### Squad building economy
- Build around **2–3 league/nation cores** for easy chemistry (see `05`).
- Spend coins on **PlayStyles+ at key positions** before chasing big names.
- **Do SBCs with untradeable fodder**; buy SBC players if cheaper than packing.
- **Objectives & milestones** quietly hand you strong cards — do the free ones.

### Rush (FUT/Clubs 5v5)
- Small-sided: **spacing and through balls** dominate. Don't bunch up.
- Pick a **pacey, high-dribbling** player; the **blue captain pick** can swing a
  match — grab a meta forward or a lockdown CB.
- Defending is about **angles and interceptions**, not slide tackles (one mistimed
  tackle in 5v5 = a goal).

---

## Career Mode (Manager)

### Pre-season
- Set your **identity + custom tactics** (from `02`) and drill them in friendlies so
  the AI squad learns the roles.
- Establish a **first XI + role assignments** so player growth funnels correctly.

### In-season management
- **Rotate** to manage fatigue and injuries; sharpness matters — undercooked stars
  underperform.
- **Sliders/instructions per opponent:** sit deeper vs stronger sides, press weaker
  ones.
- **Sign 2–3 wonderkids per window**, loan them for game time, sell fringe players
  to balance the books (see `05`).
- **Youth academy:** scout cheap regions, promote gems — a near-free pipeline of
  sell-on value and squad depth.

### Player Career
- Pick a **club + role** that gives you minutes; request a position that suits your
  growth plan.
- Hit the **match objectives** and training to grow attributes toward your role.

---

## Pro Clubs / Clubs (11v11)

- **Communication + roles** win. Assign positions and a shared tactic; don't all
  chase the ball.
- Build your **Virtual Pro** around one archetype (e.g. Explosive Inside Forward,
  Lengthy CB) and the PlayStyles that role needs — don't spread points thin.
- **Hold your position and shape**; clubs games are lost by everyone defending the
  same blade of grass. Trust teammates and cover zones.
- **Keeper + CBs** are massively undervalued — a disciplined defense wins divisions.

---

## Carrying principles across every mode
1. **Defend as a shape, attack on your terms.**
2. **One tactic, mastered**, beats five you half-know.
3. **PlayStyles+ and role fit** over raw overall.
4. **Manage the game state** — mentality and subs are tactics too.
5. **Practice mechanics daily** (`06`); they're the multiplier on everything else.
